Sushi

Description

Sushi consists of sweet vinegared rice surrounding fish or vegetables. The whole is then wrapped in seaweed.

Ingredients

  • Dry seaweed
  • Rice in ample quantity
  • Raw fish or vegetables

Steps

  1. Lay a square of dry seaweed on the table
  2. Spread 0.5cm of rice on the seaweed
  3. Place a line of the other ingredients in the middle of the aforementioned rice
